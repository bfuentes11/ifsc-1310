    Thoughts on Progressive Enhancement

    Progessive Enhancement is the process of seperating your content from your css and javascript in order to provide the best experience for the user and web browser. Progressive Enhancment also tries to obtain accessbility and peak functionality in all old and new browsers. Progressive Enhancement is primarily focused on the content aspect of the webpage. The entire reason web pages were ever created was for the content. The Progressive Enhancement point of view makes you start with the base of your webpage (HTML) then shifts your focus to the presentation of your HTML (CSS). Finally, it shifts your focus to the Javascript portion of your webpage. Progessive Enhancement is important to modern web technology due to the fact that content is the most important part of the web page.
